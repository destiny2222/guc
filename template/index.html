Prof. C.u.c ugorji

A Full Professor of Linguistics and English Language. has worked as a teacher, lecturer, and researcher for more than 30 years in Africa and Europe. An eminent collaborator in institution-funded research groups, including the International Corpus of English (ICE) hosted in Germany, especially in eliciting data and editing the electronic corpora documentation; and the international Corpho Group (corpus phonology group) I have contributed to English studies in international contexts, including Germany and Poland.
